Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for weddings and 2-4 weeks for other events to ensure availability.

Do you travel for destination events?

Yes! We love destination events. Travel fees may apply depending on location and duration.

How long does it take to receive photos/videos?

Wedding galleries are typically delivered within 4-6 weeks, while event coverage is usually ready in 2-3 weeks.

What's included in your packages?

Each package is customized to your needs. Basic packages include coverage, editing, and digital delivery. Premium options add extras like albums, extra hours, and second shooters.
